Methodology

Cash Flow

Monthly cash flow is calculated as rental income minus operating expenses and mortgage payments.

Cash-on-Cash Return

Cash-on-cash return measures the annual pre-tax cash flow relative to the total cash invested:

CoC = Annual Cash Flow / Total Cash Invested

Cap Rate

The capitalization rate measures property profitability independent of financing:

Cap Rate = Net Operating Income / Property Value

Break-even Rent

Break-even rent is the minimum rent required to cover all expenses, including mortgage payments.

Amortization

The amortization schedule shows how each payment is split between interest and principal over time.
